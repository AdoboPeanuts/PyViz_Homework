Dashboard Panel

The panel consists of five tabs, and each account contains different plot graphs which were related to different dwelling types in the Toronto area from 2001 to 2016

1.  The Home tab reveals the neighbourhood map.  The map plots the different average home values in Greater Toronto Area.

2 . The Yearly Market Analysis tab reveals the historical data of different dwelling types available in the Greater Toronto area from 2001 to 2016. 

3.  Shelter Cost vs Home Value tab plots the trends of monthly shelter cost in relationship with the average house value in the Greater Toronto Area from 2001 to 2016. 

4.  The Neighbourhood Analysis tab plots the different house values per neighbourhood in the Greater Toronto Area. It also identifies the most expensive neighbourhood in the GTA from 2001 to 2016. 

5.  The Top Expensive Neighbourhood tab takes an in-depth look at the top ten most expensive neighbourhoods in the Greater Toronto Area from 2001 to 2016. The tab shows the average house values per neighbourhood and the average monthly shelter cost from 2001 to 2016.
